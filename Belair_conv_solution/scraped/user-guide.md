### Frequently asked questions about making online payments 

Still have questions about paying your insurance bill online? We're here to help.

### 1 - Can I use either the Client Centre or the belairdirect app to pay my insurance bill?

Yes. You can use whichever method best suits you to pay your insurance bill. If you prefer to pay your bills on your computer, you can do so through the Client Centre. If you're on the go, you can use your smartphone and pay your insurance bill through the belairdirect app. 

### 2 - What methods of payment can I use to pay my insurance bill through the app or Client Centre?

When paying your belairdirect insurance bill online, you'll need to use a credit or a debit card. belairdirect accepts Visa, MasterCard and American Express as forms of payment. Unfortunately, belairdirect does not accept prepaid credit cards at this time. 

### 3 - What should I do if an error message pops up?

An error message pops up in 1of 3 scenarios: 

- When an answer box requiring mandatory information hasn't been filled out before clicking "Submit Payment". Be sure to fill out all the required information before confirming the payment.

- When invalid credit or debit card information has been entered. Verify that the card number, name (as written on the card), expiration date and CVV number are all correct. Ensure that your remaining credit limit is greater than the payment you're making.

- When there's an error in processing the payment. If this error persists, you'll simply need to try again later. 

### 4 - What is the minimum amount I need to pay?

The field "Amount Due Immediately" contains your balance due. You can modify your payment to any amount greater than $0, but it must be equal to, or less than the amount due indicated. 

### 5 - Can I schedule a payment?

Good news! You can now post-date online payments using the same payment process as previously available in the Client Centre

### 6 - What happens if my scheduled payment fails?

If your payment does not go through on the scheduled date, a second email will be sent to inform you about the failed payment. You will be invited to connect to your Client Centre to make your payment with another credit or debit card.

### How a home insurance claim works

When you file a home insurance claim, a claims representative will take you through an easy, step-by-step process until your claim is completed. If your home requires repairs, you can choose from our Rely Network of professional property restoration contractors to deliver quality work, guaranteed.

Here's how the claim process typically works. Remember, each incident is unique and your claim may be different according to your specific case and coverage.

### For theft

- Open a claim
 
- Information is gathered about the incident
 
- Your claim is assessed for payment
 
- We issue payment to you and/or replace the stolen items
 
- Your claim is closed

### For a major loss (e.g. due to water damage)

- Open a claim
 
- If needed, we will help you temporarily relocate
 
- Meet with a claims adjuster
 
- Assessment and estimation of damage
 
- We issue payment to you, replace lost items or have your home repaired
 
- You return home
 
- Your claim is closed

### For a minor loss (e.g. due to sewer backup water damage, vandalism, hail or wind)

- Open a claim
 
- Assessment and estimation of damage
 
- If repairs are needed, you choose a supplier
 
- We issue payment to you, replace lost items or help you repair your home
 
- Your claim is closed

### Frequently asked questions about travel insurance claims

### What is a travel insurance claim? 

A travel insurance claim is when you require reimbursement for an unexpected medical emergency while travelling, or when you are unable to leave or return from your trip as planned. The most common types of claims are for medical emergencies, trip cancellations, delayed flights, and lost luggage

### How do I file a travel insurance claim?

Give our partner Global Excel a call and we will get your claim started right away, no matter where you are in the world. Call 1-800-709-3420 in Canada and the USA or 1-819-566-1002 to call collect. Visit www.globalexcel.com/claimcentre for more information.

### What documents do I need to provide when filing a claim?

Depending on the type of claim you’re filing, you may need to provide medical documentation, a baggage claim form, your travel itinerary, an incident report, or receipts for items purchased while waiting for delayed or lost baggage. For more information, please review the Global Excel portal.

### What should I do if my baggage is delayed or lost?

Begin your insurance claim as soon as possible to avoid any delays in processing and reimbursement. If your plan covers the replacement cost for essential items while you wait for your belongings, make sure to keep all the necessary receipts.
