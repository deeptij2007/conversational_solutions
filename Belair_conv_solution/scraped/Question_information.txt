What is a soft credit check
A soft credit check provides a basic overview of your credit report without impacting your score. With this information, your price will reflect our best offer and you could save up to 25% on your car insurance with a soft credit check.

By selecting Yes, you agree that belairdirect may collect and use your credit information from consumer reporting agencies. If you become a customer, this information will be updated periodically to determine your future price (insurance premium) and any potential savings.

For more information, visit the Insurance Bureau of Canada's guide: All about credit information and insurance.
